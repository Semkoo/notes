Regex to keep in mind
------

<dl>
  <dt>Single Email</dt>
  <dd>Example: someone@email.com</dd>
  <dd>/^(([^<>()\[\]\.,;:\s@\”]+(\.[^<>()\[\]\.,;:\s@\”]+)*)|(\”.+\”))@(([^<>()[\]\.,;:\s@\”]+\.)+[^<>()[\]\.,;:\s@\”]{2,})$/i</dd>

  <dt>Multiple Email</dt>
  <dd>Example: someone@email.com,someone1@email.com</dd>  
  <dd></dd>
</dl>
